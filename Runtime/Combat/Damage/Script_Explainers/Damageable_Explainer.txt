SCRIPT: Damageable.cs
FILE: Runtime/Combat/Damage/Damageable.cs
NAMESPACE: GameplayMechanicsUMFOSS.Combat

Purpose:
This is the default concrete implementation of `IDamageable` used by the boomerang sample targets.

Key points:
- `TakeDamage()` clamps the applied value to remaining health, so overkill damage does not produce misleading results.
- `ApplyKnockback()` uses the hit payload instead of hard-coded force directions, which keeps the receiver reusable.
- `OnHealthChanged` and `OnDeath` support direct local reactions, while `EventBus.Publish(...)` supports global reactions.
- The hit flash uses `MaterialPropertyBlock`, which avoids creating unique material instances for every target renderer.
