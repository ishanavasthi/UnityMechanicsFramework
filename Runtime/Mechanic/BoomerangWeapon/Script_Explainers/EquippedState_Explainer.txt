SCRIPT: EquippedState.cs
FILE: Runtime/Mechanic/BoomerangWeapon/Scripts/States/EquippedState.cs
NAMESPACE: GameplayMechanicsUMFOSS.Combat.States

Purpose:
This state represents the weapon resting in the player's hand and waiting for input.

Key points:
- `SetKinematic(true)` disables free-body motion before the weapon is reattached to the hand socket.
- The collider is disabled here because the equipped weapon should not deal accidental damage while idle.
- `SetParent(ctx.HandSocket)` plus zeroed local pose makes the catch result deterministic and easy to author in the prefab.
- It clears stale collision and hurtbox references so the next throw starts from a clean state.
