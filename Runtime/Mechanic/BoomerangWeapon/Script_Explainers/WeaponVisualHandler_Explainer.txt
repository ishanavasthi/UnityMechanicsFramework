FILE: Runtime/Mechanic/BoomerangWeapon/Scripts/WeaponVisualHandler.cs
NAMESPACE: GameplayMechanicsUMFOSS.Combat

Purpose:
This plain C# helper owns the weapon's presentation spin.

Key points:
- The spin is applied to a child visual pivot, not the weapon root, so the root transform stays reliable for travel direction and parenting logic.
- `Spin()` rotates in `Space.Self`, which makes the visual motion consistent regardless of world orientation.
- `ResetRotation()` is used when the weapon catches or embeds so the mesh never stays frozen at an awkward mid-spin angle.
