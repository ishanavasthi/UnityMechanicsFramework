SCRIPT: ThrownState.cs
FILE: Runtime/Mechanic/BoomerangWeapon/Scripts/States/ThrownState.cs
NAMESPACE: GameplayMechanicsUMFOSS.Combat.States

Purpose:
This state runs the outgoing flight after the player throws the weapon.

Key points:
- `SetParent(null)` detaches the weapon from the player rig so physics can control it independently.
- `AddForce(..., ForceMode.VelocityChange)` gives a predictable launch regardless of rigidbody mass.
- The outgoing speed is reshaped every frame with `throwCurve`, which makes the flight feel tuned rather than purely ballistic.
- `HitDetector.CheckOverlap()` applies damage during flight without requiring trigger colliders on every target.
- The state transitions to `Embedded` on a valid stuck-layer collision or on a hurtbox hit when `EmbedInTargets` is enabled.
- It auto-recalls at `MaxRange` so the weapon never gets stranded.
