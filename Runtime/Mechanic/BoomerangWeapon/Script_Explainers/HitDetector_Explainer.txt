SCRIPT: HitDetector.cs
FILE: Runtime/Mechanic/BoomerangWeapon/Scripts/HitDetector.cs
NAMESPACE: GameplayMechanicsUMFOSS.Combat

Purpose:
This helper handles overlap-based target detection and one-hit-per-flight damage application.

Key points:
- `hitTargets` prevents the same collider from taking damage every frame while the weapon overlaps it.
- `ClearHitTargets()` resets flight history when a new outgoing or return phase begins.
- `cachedRadius` is captured from the weapon collider so overlap checks still work even when the collider is disabled during other states.
- `CheckOverlap()` chooses between throw damage and return damage based on `isReturnFlight`.
- `GetComponentInParent<IDamageable>()` supports hurtbox colliders that live below a larger enemy root.
