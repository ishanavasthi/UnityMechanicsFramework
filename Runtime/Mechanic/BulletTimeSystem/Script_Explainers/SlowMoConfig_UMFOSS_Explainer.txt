FILE: Runtime/Mechanic/BulletTimeSystem/Scripts/SlowMoConfig_UMFOSS.cs
NAMESPACE: GameplayMechanicsUMFOSS.Systems

Purpose:
ScriptableObject profile for one slow motion feel.

Key points:
- `timeScale`, `enterDuration`, and `exitDuration` define the pacing of the effect itself.
- `enterCurve` and `exitCurve` let designers tune whether slow motion eases in softly or snaps in aggressively.
- `audioPitchScale` keeps sound in sync with the slowed visuals.
- `resourceCost`, `resourceDrainRate`, and `resourceRechargeRate` let the same runtime system behave like a free toggle, a stamina ability, or a short ultimate.
